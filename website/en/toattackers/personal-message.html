First of all


You can heal the aftereffects of sexual violence.

If you're embarrassed by these pages, remind yourself that you've already been through the hardest part of sexual violence or something like that. You've survived an overwhelming ordeal simply beyond control. The sexual violence that gnawed at you has simultaneously provided the inner resources necessary for healing. There is one thing anyone who survives sexual violence can confidently declare. It is the fact that he has power. Also, because they understand what is needed for healing, the power can lead directly to the destination of healing. One woman said: "No one will touch me anymore."

Writing training: aftereffects

Describe how sexual violence is affecting you now. What feelings do you continue to have about self-respect, your work, your relationship with others, and your sexuality? How is your life suffering and what limitations are you facing?

Describe the power developed by sexual violence. Think of the things that were very important for you to survive. What are the characteristics that made you survive? Patience? Flexibility? Self-fulfillment? Describe your power with a confident mind.
