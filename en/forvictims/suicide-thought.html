When you're really in a bad mood, you want to die. The pain is too great, too self-loathing and too deep in fear to really want to live. These are your real feelings, and they are irresistibly important. In addition, should not act in accordance with that feeling that important. It's okay to feel you're devastated, but don't hurt yourself.

We've already lost too many women. Too many adult and child victims committed suicide in despair without sufficient support. We can't lose any more. We can't lose you. You are well worth living.

You've been taught to face anger inward. What's so bad about wanting to die is that you have that kind of anger inside you. The anger should have been rescheduled for those who had hurt you so badly young. The closer you get to that anger, the more self-loathing will be diluted. You'll want to keep your life going.

Ask for help immediately if you want to kill yourself or start feeling self-inflicted. If you are suffering from suicide, it is important to get help from a competent therapist. You should also know the phone number of the suicide prevention hotline in advance. Store in a conspicuous and accessible place with the therapist's phone number. That way, you can find the number even in the most difficult situations.
